## ü¶Ü Travel Tips from Diesel Goose

### Packing Efficiently
- **Carry-on only** for trips under 5 days
- **One bag philosophy** ‚Äî Roll clothes, minimize shoes
- **Tech pouch** ‚Äî All cables, chargers, adapters in one place
- **Backup essentials** ‚Äî In carry-on: 1 change of clothes, medications

### Airport Hacks
- TSA PreCheck/Global Entry (company reimburses)
- Download offline maps before landing
- Airport lounges: Priority Pass for long layovers
- Book morning flights (fewer delays)

### Productivity on the Road
- Batch meetings in same time zone when possible
- Use travel time for deep work (planes = no distractions)
- Buffer day after return (no meetings) for recovery
- Stay hydrated, move regularly, prioritize sleep

### Crypto Travel Tips
- Keep hardware wallet in separate bag from laptop
- Use Xumm for XRP payments where accepted
- Have backup seed phrase (securely stored)
- Check local crypto regulations before travel
